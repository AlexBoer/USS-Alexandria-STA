Relja Log 2.1Stardate 68063 - Alexandria Log 2.1 - Orion Heist
While on shore leave, we were recruited to track down a stolen relic that seems to be related to the incident on Bacchus-3. Alex and I played good cop bad cop with a local Ferengi fight promoter and got a lead on some Orions that had likely stolen the relic.

I quickly called for the engineering team to prep the ship for departure. The Capt helped us scramble an emergency crew together and head out for the fleeing Orion ship. We managed to catch it, but of course the pirates denied any knowledge of the relic.

At that point the Orion ship began to implode. Concerned for the safety of the Orions and needing to get the relic not only for Star Fleet but to keep it out of dangerous hand, we boarded the Orion ship. We initially made haste to try and save the crew, but quickly discovered that a crew member of the Orion ship was caught in some kind of mind meld with the artifact. Alex managed to get it to shut down and we all quickly got back to the Alexandria.

Of course the Romulans behind this whole fiasco took that moment to decloak and make us aware they wanted the relic. please. without the please.
Relja Log 2.2Stardate 68084 - Alexandria Log 2.2 - Boarded
After securing the relic from the Orions, we sent their poor officer to sick bay while we took the relic to the Lounge to review our options. The Romulans began attacking so we quickly shifted to Engineering to try and use the Relic to shore up the shields or push the Romulans away.

While attempting to do this the Romulans were able to fully take down the shields long enough to board us. I focused on getting the relic online to secure it and to save the ship. (The Mission Comes First). With Eelara's help I managed to complete the task and pushed the Romulans away. then we used the device to duck into subspace to give us time to repair the ship.

Unfortunately, some ancient empire messaged and said they were on their way to say hello, and it didnt sound like a friendly hello.
